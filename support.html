Frequently asked questions

How do I play with a friend online?
Open Friends, set a username, add your friend by their username, then tap Play next to their name to invite them into a match. They'll see your invite and tap Join.

Do I need an account?
No. You play anonymously — just choose a username. Note: reinstalling the app starts a fresh profile.

How do I reset my stats?
Settings → Stats → Reset Stats. This clears your wins and leaderboard ranks.

How does the scoring / leaderboard work?
Games against other people count toward the Global and Friends leaderboards; games vs the computer appear on your personal Single Play board.

I see ads — can I turn them off?
The app is free and supported by ads. You can limit ad personalization in your device settings — on iOS via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, on Android via Settings → Privacy → Ads.

How do I learn the rules?
Tap How to Play on the home screen, or start the built-in Tutorial round.
